About YSC

Youngsters Sports Club (YSC) Bangalore is dedicated to promoting throwball as a competitive sport in Karnataka. We organize premier tournaments that bring together talented players from across the region.

Our mission is to provide a platform for athletes to showcase their skills, develop sportsmanship, and compete at the highest level. Join us in celebrating the spirit of throwball!
